Seaart.ai Product Information

What is Seaart.ai?

Seaart.ai (also known as Sea Art AI) is a leading AI image generation tool designed for artistic creation and visual content development. The platform leverages advanced artificial intelligence technology to transform text descriptions into high-quality images, supporting various styles and creative needs. Beyond basic image generation, Seaart AI integrates image editing, AI video generation, and AI character creation capabilities, providing creators with comprehensive creative solutions.

How to use Seaart.ai?

  • Registration and Login

    Visit the Seaart.ai website and click the "Login" button in the top right corner to create an account. New users receive free credits to start their creative journey.

  • Create AI Artwork

    Click the "Generate" button on the interface, select high-performance models like Seaart Ultra 2.0, input detailed image descriptions (e.g., "beach at sunset, Studio Ghibli style"), adjust parameters to optimize results, click the "Generate" button, and wait a few seconds to get your AI artwork.

  • Explore Advanced Features

    • Image Variations: Upload existing images to create variations in different styles
    • ControlNet: Precisely control image composition and structural elements
    • AI Character Creation: Design virtual characters for use in various scenarios
    • Swift AI Tools: One-click image editing and enhancement tools

  • Community Participation

    Join Seaart AI's active community to share your work, gain inspiration, and participate in various creative activities.

Seaart.ai FAQ

Is Seaart.ai free to use?

Seaart AI offers a basic free plan where users receive a certain amount of credits daily for creation. High-frequency users can choose paid subscription plans for additional features and more generation quotas.

How to achieve better creative results?

Use detailed, specific prompts; select AI models suitable for your target style; utilize the platform's built-in prompt examples as references; use ControlNet for precise control over image composition; apply Swift AI tools for post-production optimization.
