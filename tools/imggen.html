Frequently Asked Questions About ImgGen AI

Is ImgGen AI free to use?

ImgGen AI is a product that can be used for free.

How detailed should the text-to-image description be?

Detailed and specific descriptions yield more accurate results. Ideal descriptions should include subject, environment, colors, lighting, and style elements. For example, "orange tabby cat in a sunny garden with blurred purple flowers in the background, shallow depth of field effect" produces more artistic images than simply "a cat." ImgGen AI can also understand brief descriptions and generate impressive results.

How is the quality of upscaled images?

ImgGen AI's super-resolution technology outperforms traditional methods by intelligently reconstructing details and avoiding pixelation and blurring. It works well for photos, illustrations, and documents, maintaining satisfactory clarity even at 4x enlargement. Only extremely low-resolution original images might not recover all details, but results still surpass conventional enlargement methods.
