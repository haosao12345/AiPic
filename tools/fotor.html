Fotor AI Product Information

What is Fotor?

Fotor is your ultimate AI photo editing toolkit: remove backgrounds, generate photos, de-blur images, AI photo editor, AI image generator, AI avatars, and more! No need to switch between or use complex AI tools, and it's free! Fotor integrates multiple AI image processing functions into one platform, allowing users to create and edit professional-level images without learning complex image editing techniques. Whether you're a professional designer, content creator, or ordinary user, Fotor can meet your various image processing needs.

How to use Fotor?

Using Fotor is very simple, even beginners can quickly get started:

  1. Visit the Fotor official website
  2. Create an account, choose the free version or paid plan
  3. Select the function you need: AI image generator, photo editing, background removal, etc.
  4. Upload images for editing or use the AI image generator to create new images
  5. Save and share your work after editing

Fotor's interface design is clean and intuitive, new users can easily master various functions without professional training. The online editor supports all modern browsers, and it also offers Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android applications, allowing you to create anytime, anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions about Fotor

Fotor offers free versions and paid subscription plans. The free version includes basic editing functions, while advanced features like the AI image generator require a paid subscription plan. Fotor's paid plans are reasonably priced, providing flexible options for users with different budgets.

Do I need professional skills to use Fotor?

No. Fotor is designed to be simple and intuitive, new users can quickly get started through built-in tutorials. Fotor's AI technology can automatically complete many complex editing tasks, creating outstanding visual effects without requiring professional knowledge or skills.
